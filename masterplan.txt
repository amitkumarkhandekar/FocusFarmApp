1. App Overview & Objectives

FocusFarm is a mobile productivity app for students that helps them build consistent study habits through a playful, game-like farming experience.

Instead of growing trees (like Forest), users rear animals by completing focus goals. Over time, they build an infinite farm that visually represents their discipline, effort, and progress.

Primary Objectives

Make studying feel fun and emotionally rewarding

Encourage daily consistency without guilt or punishment

Provide a strong solo experience first, with a clear path to social features

Offer a student-friendly monetization model without paywalls

User-defined (flexible duration)

Pause allowed

Session continues even if app is closed

Other apps are blocked during an active session
